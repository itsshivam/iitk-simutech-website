    About Us

    SimuTech is a group of students in Chemical Engineering department of IIT-Kanpur. It provides a platform for students to pursue their interest in theoretical, practical and simulation projects related to chemical engineering and other fields. Here in SimuTech group, we give you immense opportunities to know the astonishing world of chemical technology, to think in new directions and to innovate. It’s a perfect place to shape your imagination real and pursue your ideas with freedom. Currently, we are a team of 29 undergraduates from different academic years, where seniors and juniors are working together on different projects. We organise the workshop on softwares like Matlab and Aspen plus .We have also done projects in collaboration with organisation like Nariphaltan. Very soon, we intend to participate in the prestigious national level chemical engineering competitions. Apart from it, we do have a lot of fun in get together sessions.
